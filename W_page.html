About NERISTConnect

NERISTConnect is a dynamic platform designed to bring together the vibrant community of NERIST (North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology). It serves as a comprehensive digital hub where students, faculty, and staff can seamlessly connect, communicate, and collaborate. From academic resources and real-time notifications to campus events and essential services, NERISTConnect simplifies and enhances the college experience by making information accessible at the fingertips of every user. The platform prioritizes user-friendliness, modern design, and personalized experiences, creating a supportive online environment that mirrors the rich culture and collaborative spirit of NERIST.
